Privacy Policy

At Enyecopypaste.com, accessible from our main domain, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by our platform and how we use it.

Log Files & Analytics

We follow a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected by log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, and referring/exit pages. This data is not linked to any personally identifiable information.

Google Advertising & Cookies

Google is one of the third-party vendors on our site. It uses cookies, known as DART cookies, to serve ads to our site visitors based upon their visit to our platform and other sites on the internet. Visitors may choose to decline the use of DART cookies by visiting the Google ad and content network Privacy Policy.
